In today's stressful times, anxiety and hopelessness have become the norm rather than the exception. Having a place to safely explore mixed emotions and responses to chaotic times can be invaluable. I use holistic and experiential oriented approaches to address the mental, physical, and spiritual components of struggles. I aim to bring compassionate curiosity while helping you uncover the underlying emotional patterns and beliefs contributing to symptoms.

Informed by depth and relational psychology, I look at your dreams, memories, and beliefs to uncover root causes and the personal as well as collective nature of symptoms. This dynamic approach has the ability for lasting transformation. When self-awareness and acceptance are gained through insight, a sustained freedom to behave differently is possible.

My approach weaves together depth psychology, Jungian analysis, art therapy, and somatic practices. I'm especially drawn to working with dreams, symbols, and creative expression as pathways to understanding ourselves more deeply. Whether you're navigating life transitions, healing from trauma, or seeking greater meaning and purpose, I walk alongside you with curiosity, warmth, and respect for your unique journey.

I work with adults and children, offering a space where the unspoken can find voice through words, art, play, or simply being present together.
